Executive Summary

Aroma Ceylon is a luxury Sri Lankan spice brand producing 100% natural curry powders for meat and soya dishes. Driven by the motto “Tradition Regale to Perfection”, the brand focuses on freshness, artisan small-batches, eco-friendly packaging and a high-quality sensory profile.
